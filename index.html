PERMAN & HIS FRIENDS

Perman's friends include Sumire Hoshino, a girl who has a crush on Mitsuo; Booby, a clumsy bird who often causes trouble; and Patalliro, a mischievous alien who frequently visits Earth. Together, they go on exciting adventures and work to protect their town from various threats. Perman also has a rival named Ganko-chan, who can transform into a superhero like Perman. Despite their rivalry, Ganko-chan and Perman often team up to save the day.


PERMAN'S CRUSH

PERMAN'S CRUSH

Perman is a superhero with the ability to fly, super strength, and shape-shifting powers. He is known for his kind-hearted and brave nature, always fighting for justice and protecting his friends and town. His loyal sidekick, Pako, often accompanies him on his adventures and helps him in times of need. Despite Pako's mischievous nature, Perman values their friendship above all else and even has a crush on her. Together, they make a great team and are loved by both children and adults alike.
